# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience
for everyone who participates in the SecretScout community.

This project is focused on **defensive security**, education, and prevention-first tooling.
All interactions should reflect professionalism, respect, and good faith collaboration.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in discussions
- Providing constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and users
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or inflammatory comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Sharing real secrets, credentials, or sensitive data
- Encouraging offensive or malicious use of security tools

---

## Security-Specific Expectations

Because SecretScout is a **defensive security tool**:

- ❌ Do NOT share real API keys, tokens, passwords, or private keys
- ❌ Do NOT request or promote offensive usage
- ✅ Use **dummy values only** in issues, tests, and documentation
- ✅ Follow responsible disclosure practices

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior
they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- A warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from the project
- Removal of content or contributions

Decisions will be made at the maintainers’ discretion, with the goal of maintaining
a healthy and productive community.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any other community interaction related to SecretScout

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately
to the project maintainers via GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.
